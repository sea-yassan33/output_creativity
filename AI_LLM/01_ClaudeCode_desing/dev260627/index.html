While You're Here

Days Without an Agenda

Dawn

Forest Bathing at First Light

A guided walk through the old-growth section of the property, moving slowly enough to hear the forest settle into the morning. No phones. No destination.

Table

Foraged & Fire-Cooked

Our kitchen team forages the surrounding woodland each morning. Dinner is a single long table — all guests together — lit by beeswax candles, served over open flame.

Water

The Spring Pool

Fed by a natural spring at 14°C, the pool is unheated by design. Cold immersion is followed by a cedar sauna built into the hillside. Open from 5am. Private by arrangement.
